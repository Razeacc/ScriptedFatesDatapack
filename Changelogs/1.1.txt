## Additions
RECYCLING
- Recipe for turning bows, fishing rods, or crossbows into sticks
- Crafting an arrow BELOW a bone breaks the feather to recycle 1 flint
- Crafting an arrow BELOW a bone breaks the flint to recycle 1 feather
- 6 Sticks in a Y shape makes a "Dead Bush Prop"
- Dead Bushes can be crafted back into 6 sticks 
- 4 Leather Equipment can be recycled into 5 leather
- 1 Player Head + 1 Echo Shard = 10 "Beyonder Essence"
PYROKINETICS
- Sponge Drying Recipe (1 Wet Sponge + 2 Blaze Powder = 1 Dry Sponge)
- Blast Furnace Compatibility with Raw Ore Block Smelting (60% faster than smelting)
- Food Burning in the Smelter or Smoker (gives "Burnt Food" charcoal, which is edible...)
HYDRATION
- Sponge Wettening Recipe (3 Sponges + 1 Nautilus Shell = 3 Wet Sponges)
- 8 Red Sand + 1 Nautilus Shell = 8 Sand
COMMON SENSE
- 8 Sand + 1 Redstone/Red Dye/Poppey = 8 Red Sand
NEW COMPRESSION DISCIPLINE
- Rapid Bone Block Crafting (3 bones or any fish => 1 bone block)
- Mass white dye conversion (4 bone blocks => 36 white dye)
MISC
- Added low chance for normal seeds, sapplings, pottery sherds to be unearthed from Sniffer Digging 

FOR DEVELOPERS
