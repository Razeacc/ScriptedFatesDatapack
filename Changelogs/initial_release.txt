# :FirmataFrontier: Cubiqua Wilds Surveypack

QoL features that add __more ways to use/reuse certain items__, all while connecting everything through lore-based game design.
Utilize several new :diamond_pickaxe: **discoveries** and :crafting: **disciplines** to broaden your gameplay!

Craft the 4 Guidebooks, fabricate an Echoing PDA, and begin your survey today.
``` ```
## Features
### :recycle: Recycling
- Nametags, Leather Equipment, and Saddles can be crafted or salvaged into leather
- Fish can be crafted into Fish Bones
- TNT can be salvaged into Sand or Gunpowder
- Wool can be scrapped into String

### :mc_phantom: Duplication
- Special Stone (Calcite, Granite, Diorite, Andesite, Tuff :skull:) Duping
- Flint and Nautilus Shell Duping
- Phantom Membrane Duplication (bugged right now, will fix next patch)

### :SpinningNautilus: Hydration
- Mass Mud Crafting
- Mass Concrete Crafting
- Mass Copper Oxidizing

### :fire: Pyrokinetics
- Raw Ore Blocks can now be smelted into Ore Blocks
- Breeze Rods can be smelted into Blaze Rods
- Leather Equipment, Saddles, and Fish can be crafted with Blaze Powder to get charcoal.

### :copper_golem_dance: Misc :amethyst:
- Charcoal Blocks
- Copper Golem crafting recipe
- Mass Copper Deoxidization
- Dark Prismarine, Red Sand, and Sculk Vein Conversion
- Fragile Amethyst Bud crafting recipe
- Harvest Flint from Gravel
