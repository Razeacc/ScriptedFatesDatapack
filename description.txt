# :FirmataFrontier: Cubiqua Wilds Surveypack

Lore-Driven QoL datapack, featuring __more ways to use/reuse certain items__ utilizing a worldbuilding-first game design philosophy. This pack's writing aims to __complement Minecraft's existing lore/world__ rather than overwrite it.
Utilize several new :diamond_pickaxe: **discoveries** and :crafting: **disciplines** to broaden your gameplay!

With the Surveypack Guidebooks and the Echoing PDA, you may start your survey today!

## - GETTING STARTED -
- Using your "Cubiqua Wilds Surveypack" for the recipe guides, craft any of the 4 Guidebooks using a book and the Discipline's Catalyst.
- Eventually, craft Mask Maker's Echoing PDA to access more intermediate/advanced recipes and features of the datapack.

Guidebooks and the Echoing PDA are fully readable in-game. They will expand alongside future updates.
